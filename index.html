Matrix #412

Matrix #412 is a short sound-driven animation, animated using Houdini.
Click on the image or the title above to watch the full version.

Through this animation, I tried to visualise some noise by analyzing its FFT spectrum, pitch, volume and other stuff. I always have this kind of obsession on noise and its abstract visual representation, and would like to explore more about the relationship between them in depth.


Strange Cellular Pleasure

Click on the above frame to start or pause.
This sketch includes sound, so you may want to turn your speaker on or put on headphones.
This is a little experiment of "visual-driven sound generation". Sine waves with different frequencies, attack and decay time and other factors are generated and dissolved corresponing to the dynamics of the circles. The algorithm behind is inspired from the Social Dynamics model raised by Rainer Hegselmann and Andreas Flache in 1998, as well as the Cellular Automata Based Modelling.
